Pitch

Description

Do you think you can complete the roads mysteriously built in outer space? It is time to put your driving skills to the test in this difficult game that requires skills, patience and perseverance. Put on the mantle of a cosmic rider and complete the beautiful roads in outer space.

History

I was a huge fan of video games when I was a little kid, in my early years of gaming I have come across a game called "Sky roads" and it was the moment that I decided that I want to make video games.

Cosmic roads is heavily inspired by Sky roads and the actual development started around late 2018 as a hobby project.

Features

  • Complete roads created in outer space.
  • Stunning outer space environments settings.
  • A game where your character doesn't get better, you get better.
